# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Everyone who participates in this project — whether by reporting bugs, suggesting features, submitting code, or joining discussions — is expected to keep things respectful and constructive.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be kind and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and receive feedback graciously.
- Stay on topic and keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project, or trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being removed or the person being blocked from the repository. Report concerns by opening a private [security advisory](../../security/advisories/new) or contacting the maintainer directly.
